We are thrilled to extend a warm invitation to the "5th International Conference on Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AIML-2025)," set to take place in London, UK, from November 17th to 18th, 2025. Hosted by United Research Forum, this event revolves around the captivating theme, “Deep Learning: Advancements & Applications.” Featuring over 30 scientific sessions, the conference aims to gather leading figures from the AI community and related fields. This event offers a golden opportunity to foster collaboration, exchange insights, and explore innovative applications within engineering and artificial intelligence. We envision this conference as a premier platform, attracting participants from across the globe, representing diverse backgrounds and interests.

Moreover, we are pleased to offer continuing professional development (CPD) credits for all attendees, ensuring that participants can enhance their professional development while staying abreast of the latest developments in the field.

United Research Forum Conferences thrive on knowledge, expertise, and exceptional individuals, establishing themselves as prominent organizers of business-to-business (B2B) events. Our commitment lies in bringing together adept speakers and industry leaders, furnishing them with strategic insights and avenues for development. We consistently prioritize pioneering trends, address contemporary subjects, and collaborate with foremost industry luminaries to curate and deliver premier events of impeccable quality. Our overarching mission encompasses orchestrating conferences, disseminating superior conference proceedings, and offering services to scientists and aspiring researchers, ensuring they stay abreast of the latest advancements across all domains of research.

Don't miss this opportunity to network with peers, exchange knowledge, and contribute to advancements in AI. Join us in London for an enriching and rewarding experience at the AIML-2025.
